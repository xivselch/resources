# Privacy Policy

XIVSelch ('we', 'us', or 'our') is an open-source, non-profit community project. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal data responsibly in accordance with the **UK GDPR**, **EU GDPR**, and the **Data Protection Act 2018**.

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Discord Authentication Data

When you sign in using **Better-Auth** with Discord, we collect:

- Your Discord User ID (serves as your user id)
- Username and avatar (for display only; these may change at any time)
- Email address (for authentication, account deletion)
- Session token (stored securely to maintain your login session)

We do **not** request or store additional Discord data.

### 1.2 XIVSelch Tool Data

We store only the minimum information required to operate the platform:

- Allied Society progress
- Custom Delivery tracking
- Feature preferences and settings
- Linked character references (as provided by you)

This data is stored in **Turso** (Ireland, EU).

### 1.3 Third-Party Game Data

The Services may retrieve publicly available FFXIV data from:

- The **Square Enix Lodestone**, via the XIVSelch API;
- **XIVAPI** and other community-maintained APIs.

We do **not** store Lodestone or external API data.
Temporary caching may be used to reduce load and maintain performance.

The XIVSelch API sends correct headers (Origin, Referrer, User-Agent) to ensure responsible access and to avoid harmful scraping.

### 1.4 Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

- Cookies or secure session tokens for login
- Local storage for user preferences and anonymous usage

Anonymous usage data remains entirely on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Authenticate your account via Discord
- Provide and operate XIVSelch tools
- Store your tool progress and preferences
- Ensure platform stability and security
- Improve functionality and performance

We do **not** sell, rent, or use your data for advertising.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR, processing is based on:

- **Contract** – providing the Services you choose to use
- **Legitimate interests** – ensuring security, preventing abuse, maintaining performance
- **Consent** – where browser storage or optional features rely on it

## 4. Storage and Security

Data is stored only in:

- **Turso (Ireland, EU)** – tool data and authentication data

Security measures include:

- Encryption in transit
- Restricted access
- No raw IP addresses stored
- No storing of Lodestone data or third-party game data

## 5. Anonymous Usage

You may use many features anonymously.
Anonymous data:

- Is stored only in your browser
- Is never transmitted to our servers
- Can be cleared at any time in your browser settings

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Rectify errors
- Request deletion
- Restrict or object to processing
- Receive a copy of your data (data portability)

Requests may be submitted through the platform or via our contact page.

## 7. Data Transfers

Data is hosted in the EU.
If content is served globally via a CDN, such transfers comply with:

- UK adequacy regulations
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), where required

## 8. Children's Privacy

Users must meet the **Discord minimum age requirement** (13+ in the UK/EU).
We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.
